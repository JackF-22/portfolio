Biography


My name is Jack Finnegan and I'm a web developer. I have been building websites for multiple years now. I am now studying Computer Science at MIT and looking to continue this interest.


I love to take complex problems and view them as a puzzle to be solved. I see websites as a road map of puzzle pieces, waiting to be fit together. I enjoy the process of combining technicals with the aesthetic of Web Development, as well as giving people and companies websites to be proud of.
